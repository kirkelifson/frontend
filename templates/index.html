About us

What we are

A free opensource Source mod based in Counter-Strike: Source, focused on bunnyhopping and surf mecanics from said game, with global laderboards to compete for the best times on multiple maps with people all across the world. We're still in development!

Features

Built-in timer. Global laderboards. (Checkpoints?)

The team

Leaded by Source runner Gocnak, we're a group of volunteers who spend free time developing the mod.
